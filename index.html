Pre & Post Cruise Travel Options

🇮🇹 Italy Pre-Cruise (3-4 nights)

Rome Option: Arrive 2-3 days early. Stay near Vatican or Spanish Steps. Visit Colosseum, Vatican Museums, Trevi Fountain. Fly into FCO airport.
Sicily Option: Fly into Catania. 3 nights exploring Mount Etna, Taormina, and Palermo before cruising from nearby Italian ports.
Florence/Tuscany: If cruising from Livorno port, spend 2-3 days in Florence and Tuscan wine country.

🇩🇪 Germany Post-Cruise (4-5 nights)

Munich & Bavaria: Fly from Rome/Barcelona to Munich. Visit Neuschwanstein Castle, beer gardens, day trip to Salzburg.
Berlin Option: Historical sites, museums, Brandenburg Gate. Easy train connections to other European cities.
Rhine Valley: River cruise or drive through wine regions. Frankfurt is a good flying-home hub.
