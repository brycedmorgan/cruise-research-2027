Pre & Post Cruise Travel Options

🇮🇹 Italy Pre-Cruise (3-4 nights)

Rome Option: Arrive 2-3 days early. Stay near Vatican or Spanish Steps. Visit Colosseum, Vatican Museums, Trevi Fountain. Fly into FCO airport.
Sicily Option: Fly into Catania. 3 nights exploring Mount Etna, Taormina, and Palermo before cruising from nearby Italian ports.
Florence/Tuscany: If cruising from Livorno port, spend 2-3 days in Florence and Tuscan wine country.

🇩🇪 Germany Post-Cruise (4-5 nights)

Munich & Bavaria: Fly from Rome/Barcelona to Munich. Visit Neuschwanstein Castle, beer gardens, day trip to Salzburg.
Berlin Option: Historical sites, museums, Brandenburg Gate. Easy train connections to other European cities.
Rhine Valley: River cruise or drive through wine regions. Frankfurt is a good flying-home hub.

🎯 Bogey's No-BS Recommendations

Best Bang for Your Buck: Norwegian Dawn from Barcelona - $1,209 PP inside cabins. You'll feel fancy without breaking the bank. Perfect for couples who want flexibility and don't need to impress the neighbors back home.
The Sweet Spot: Princess Enchanted from Rome - $1,114 PP inside. Classy without being stuffy. Fewer screaming kids, more couples actually talking to each other over dinner. Utah mom approved.
Go Big or Go Home: Celebrity Ascent (10 nights) - $2,448 PP oceanview. This is where Utah moms discover they actually DO deserve to be pampered. Italy/Greece/Croatia + foot massages = life changed forever.
Baller Status: Oceania Nautica (Istanbul to Rome) - $3,285 PP but includes gratuities and drinks. Small ship luxury where the biggest decision is which amazing restaurant to try tonight. You'll come home spoiled for life.
Real Talk: Book by May 2026 or kiss the good cabins goodbye. Mediterranean summer cruises sell out to people who actually know what they're doing. Don't be the couple stuck with the cabin next to the elevator.
